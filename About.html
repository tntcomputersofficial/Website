About us

Photo by Fancycrave.com from Pexels

-- Our objective --

To provide the customer with a flexible alternative to buying Gaming computers. Working with your budget but also providing you with the best of the best. When you contact us to build you a computer we help you pick out the right stuff for what you want. We have built many computers over the years and want to provide our experience as a service to you! Our passion is gaming so if that's what you want out of your computer we have you covered! Do you need to upgrade what you currently have in your office? No problem! We know what work load requires what and how we can speed up your buisness but not make your setup over powered!

The Process

First you send us an email
using the "Request a build" page


We then contact you to learn
more about what you need out of this computer


After sending you an estimate,
We make sure you're ok with the total and
send you an invoice


After paying half of the invoice we get started!
This is where you sit back and relax!


When we finish we'll email you letting you know it's done. After paying off the rest we mail the computer to you/deliver it if your local. All you have to do is connect your stuff and you're all set!
