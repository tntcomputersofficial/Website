About us

-- Our objective --

To provide the customer with a flexible alternative to buying Gaming computers. Working with your budget but also providing you with the best of the best. When you contact us to build you a computer, one of us will personally email you to get a good idea of what you want out of this machine and what your best options are.

After we talk to you we send you an estimate which can be changed to your liking. Don't like that company? This part is better anyways! Case looks to bland? This one has RGB! You decide what you want!

The Owners

We are two college students that enjoy computers and building them. Tyler has worked and has been involved in IT and building computers for roughly 5 years. 2 of which was at a job repairing and building PC's and laptops. Timothy or T.J. has worked with Tyler on several builds and shares the same knowledge of current technology and the computer building process. We're based in sunny Palm Bay, Florida.
